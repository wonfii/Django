The Mona Lisa and The Last Supper

The Last Supper

The Last Supper is one of Leonardo da Vinci's most iconic paintings, depicting the moment when Jesus announces that one of his disciples will betray him. This masterpiece is located in the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan, Italy. It showcases Leonardo's skill in capturing human emotion and the complex relationships between the figures.

Mona Lisa

The Mona Lisa is a portrait painting by Leonardo da Vinci, one of the most famous works of art in the world. It is renowned for its enigmatic expression and is displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris. The painting features a woman with an elusive smile, and its mysterious nature has captivated viewers for centuries.
